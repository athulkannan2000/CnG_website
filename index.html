Our Story

A Journey of Compassion, Innovation, and Empowerment

Our story began as a vision to make a meaningful impact on the lives of families and caregivers of children with neuro-developmental needs. The journey was set in motion through a one-year fellowship program offered by the Foundation for CfHE at IIT Hyderabad, a prestigious initiative designed to foster innovative solutions in healthcare.

Our Mission

Create and maintain a supportive environment in identifying, diagnosing, and equipping at-risk children with skills to succeed in their life

  • Assistance in Diagnosis
  • Assistance in Cognitive Development
  • Support for Physical Growth and Coordination
  • Promotion of Inclusive Learning
  • Personalized Milestone Tracking

Driving Innovation in Healthcare Technology

At Cortex N Grey Technologies, our team is powered by a unique blend of technical expertise and a deep commitment to advancing healthcare innovation. Our strength lies in our ability to merge cutting-edge technology with a passion for impactful solutions, especially in the field of child development.

Our Areas of Expertise:

  • IoT and Embedded Systems: Our team specializes in creating smart, connected devices with IoT and Embedded Systems, ensuring each product is reliable, efficient, and optimized for healthcare applications.
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning: With advanced skills in AI and ML, we design adaptive solutions that respond to real-time needs, pushing the boundaries of what healthcare technology can achieve.
  • Combined IoT and AI Synergy: The seamless integration of IoT and AI fuels our approach, enabling us to develop products that deliver meaningful insights and elevate developmental experiences for children.

Together, our team’s strengths ensure that every Cortex N Grey product is built on a foundation of technical excellence and scientific rigor, dedicated to transforming child development in healthcare.
